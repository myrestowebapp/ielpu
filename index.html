About IELPU

IELPU is a dedicated charity organization committed to alleviating poverty, providing essential education, and offering healthcare to those in need. We believe that every individual deserves a chance to thrive.

Since our inception, we have impacted thousands of lives through community-driven initiatives and unwavering global support.

What We Do

📚

Education

Providing quality education and resources to children in remote areas, unlocking their full potential.

⚕️

Healthcare

Delivering vital medical supplies, clean water, and health camps to communities lacking basic facilities.

🤝

Community Building

Empowering locals through skill development programs and sustainable livelihood projects.
